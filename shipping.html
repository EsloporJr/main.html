Shipping Policy

🚚 Shipping & Delivery 🏡

Here at Nefertum Store, we always strive to give you an enjoyable online shopping experience. To give you a better service, here's our info on shipping and delivering your orders.

  • Can Nefertum deliver anywhere in the Philipines?
    YES. Nefertum has partnered with the leading delivery services in the Philippines such as LBC, Grab, Lalamove, NinjaVan, MrSpeedy, etc.
  • How much should I pay for the shipping fee?
    NONE. Nefertum offers Free Shipping to anywhere in the Philippines.
  • I made a mistake in my delivery address. How can I edit it?
    Do not worry. Just send us a message at Nefertum_team@Gmail.Com within 24 hours and we shall change the delivery address information for you.
  • Can I purchase multiple items and have them delivered to different addresses?
    SURE. We can do that! Just talk to us about it when we call you after you checked out.
  • How long will it take for my order to be delivered?
    It will take about 3 to 5 business days. But for most of the time, it would be lesser.
  • Can I pick up my order at Nefertum office?
    Unfortunately, Nefertum is currently not offering this option.
  • Will I get a Tracking Number for my order?
    YES. You will receive an SMS or Email message with a tracking number as soon as it becomes available.
  • Do I need an account on your website to place an Order?
    No. You are most welcome to shop and buy from our store as a guest.
  • Do you offer International Shipping?
    Unfortunately, We are only taking orders within the Philippines
  • If I'm not at my home (delivery address), Can I assign another person to receive the item for me?
    YES. Since this is a Cash On Delivery, your representative may give the payment to the courier.
